Based on the latest 2024 trends, I'm implementing:

### Glassmorphism Characteristics:
- **Frosted Glass Effect**: Semi-transparent backgrounds with blur
- **Layered Depth**: Multiple translucent layers creating hierarchy
- **Light Borders**: Subtle white/light borders defining edges
- **Vivid Backgrounds**: Colorful gradients showing through glass
- **Modern & Premium**: iOS-like aesthetic (macOS Big Sur, iOS system style)

### Key Elements:
1. Blurred translucent cards for reports
2. Glass-effect header with subtle transparency
3. Floating glass buttons with depth
4. Gradient backgrounds with blur overlays
5. Smooth micro-animations

### Implementation:
- Using `@react-native-community/blur` for native iOS blur (UIVisualEffectView)
- Combining with gradient backgrounds
- Adding subtle shadows and borders
- Maintaining accessibility with good contrast

This will give your app a futuristic, clean, premium look similar to Apple's latest design language!
